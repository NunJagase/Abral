Bombing Attack

Military

The South launched a bombing raid on X-city, causing moderate damage to harbour infrastructure. Enemy bombing formation suffered moderate casualties. Our responding airforce suffered minimal casualties.

22-01-2302

Convoy Attack

Military

The South launched an attack on one of our naval convoys utilizing torpedo bombers. Transport ships suffered moderate casualties, with 2 ships sunk, 1 requiring towing back to port and an additional 2 with minor damage. No damage to military vessels. The lost cargo was primarily fish for export.

November 2301 - Present

Civil Unrest

Civil

Civil unrest has been mounting, especially in southern coastal regions closest to The South. Occasional minor protests against the war. Civilians are worried about the direction of the war, but largely continue to do their work. Overall civilian morale is moderate and in slow decline.

July 2301 - Present

Open War

Military

Open war between us and The South was declared in July 2301 and is ongoing, resulting in so far primarily naval and aerial combat, largely scattered. Neither side has deployed troops to enemy territory as of yet.

2988 - Present

Tensions with The South

Political

Tensions with The South has steadily mounted over the pasts decade and a half, resulting in ever more hostile interactings between our two nations.
