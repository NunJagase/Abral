Mercenary

There are a variety of mercenary groups, mostly small in size, that dot the Arrabithian planet. They are not generally politically affiliated, however some are not beyond taking payment to harrass specific nations. Though, their effectiveness is expected to be quite low for the effort needed to hire them.

Interplanetary Organized Private Military Company

PMC

The famous, or infamous depending on who you are, Black Cross. Mobile Infantry and Fleet who defected with the noble goal of defending colonies that the Federation refused to dedicate resources to protect. They could potentially be interested in assisting, however no doubt at a great cost. They are mostly focused around arachnids and skinnies rather than civil wars however.
